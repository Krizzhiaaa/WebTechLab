    Baguio City, on the Philippines’ Luzon island, is a mountain town of universities and resorts. Called the “City of Pines,” it’s particularly popular in summer because this place has a cool weather. Baguio City is famous for many tourist spots like the burnham lake, wright park, minesview, camp john hay, pma and many more. Baguio City is also called as the Summer Capital of the Philippines. Remember to put Baguio City into your bucket list for there many cultural heritage that can be seen and be amazed for they are preserved for the next generations.
