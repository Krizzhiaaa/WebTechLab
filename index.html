Baguio City

Baguio City, on the Philippines’ Luzon island, is a mountain town of universities and resorts. Called the “City of Pines,” it’s particularly popular in summer because this place has a cool weather. Baguio City is famous for many tourist spots like the burnham lake, wright park, minesview, camp john hay, pma and many more. Baguio City is also called as the Summer Capital of the Philippines. Remember to put Baguio City into your bucket list for there are many cultural heritage that can be seen and be amazed for they are preserved for the next generations.

Places Here In Baguio City and La Trinidad

The Mansion

The Mansion is one of the tourist spot here in Baguio City. This is the official summer residence of every President here in the Philippines. Tourist usually take picture infront of the big gate of the mansion and also infront of that mansion where there is a big long pond.

Burnham Park

Burnham park is the number 1(in our opinion) tourist place here in Baguio City where tourist sit in the grass; they eat and spend time together with their family and relatives. Kids and those who are kids at heart are also riding bikes there. Rental of bikes there are "want to sawa" — which means you can ride it without any time limit — every weekdays but in weekends it has time limit because there are alot of people who are there every weekend. People also ride mini boats with different sizes and design. Their rental are also the same as the bikes.

The Bell Church

One of the preserved culture of Baguio City. The bell church is a place where Chinese people go every chinese new year in which they pray for luck, fortune and good health. This place has alot of preserved artifacts about Buddhism and also literature of China's history.

Camp Johnhay Eco Trail

Are your friends ready for an eco walk? Well they're better be because you will have fun here in Camp Johnhay's Eco Trail. This place has alot of pine trees, small springs that flow from the top of the hill. You should also bring your camera because this place is amazing! During the rainy seasons, there are different kinds of mushrooms and flowers that grow here in this area. You will also see different crawling insects there.

The Mines View

One of the best place to go when visiting here in Baguio City is the Mines View. You will see beautiful views of the mountain formations there. There are also alot of souvenirs there and alot of things that you want to take a picture of. There are also stalls there that sells flowers and also people who sells "Inihaw na pusit" and more.

Strawberry Farm

If you want a unique experience, go in La trinidad ang do a strawberry picking with friends and relatives in the strawberry farm! You will actually pick a fresh strawberry which looks delicious than just a picture. The price of the strawberry varies because of some reason like it is wasted because of people who picked it the wrong way, attacked by insects or destroyed by global calamity. The price is determined by kilo.
