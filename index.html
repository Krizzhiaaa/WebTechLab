Festivals

Panagbenga Festival

The Panagbenga Festival is celebrated yearly during the month of February. The whole city during the month of February is surrounded with lovely flowers. During the Panagbenga Festival it shows there many floral floats and many native dances. This the time of the year that the "City of Pines" is almost surely in flower fury and fragrance over the Panagbenga Festival. It is said to be the biggest festival of Baguio City.

Lantern Parrade

Lantern Parade is an annual fiesta usually done during the first week of the month of December where it features the students of Saint Louis University (SLU) showcasing their handwork in crafting their own unique lanterns. This fiesta is a whole day activity which consists of dances, lantern carnival, and with spectacular fireworks.
