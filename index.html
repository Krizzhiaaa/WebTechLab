Visit Baguio Now!

Cool Weather

The cool weather will be the best experience that you will encounter here in Baguio City. The feeling is like entering an air-conditioned mall but better.

Summer Capital of the Philippines

Baguio City is the Summer Capital of the Philippines. This City in Northern Luzon will be the best place to go for a walk and to wander with families and relatives.

Tourist Attraction

You have all the time to visit and stroll in Baguio City because there a lot of tourist attraction that you can go. Looking for a perfect spot to take a slefie? No Problem! Baguio City is the right place! With it's preserved culture, I'm sure that you will appreciate everything here.

Festivals

Panagbenga Festival

Panagbenga Festival is one of those much awaiting festival here in the Philippines. With alot of flowers that is being showcased in all the floats in this event, I'm sure you'll appreciate it. Panagbenga is being celebrated every 4th weekend of February.

Lantern Parrade

Lantern Parades is one of the colorful events in Baguio City. Every schools in Baguio City join in this event to showcase the beauty and creativity of each school.

Local delicacies

Strawberries

Strawberries are one of the local fruits that are being sold here in Baguio City. With it's redish and yummy appearance, I'm sure you will buy alot of this and craving to taste one.

Peanut Brittle

Peanut brittle is the perfect food to buy as your souvenir food to your friends and relatives. I know that you can't resist on buying one when going here in Baguio City.

Sundot Kulangot

Have you ever wonder of what is inside of a sundot kulangot? With it's unusal name, I'm sure you'll think twice. But I assure you this food is very sweet. People love to buy this and is as something to eat while traveling.

Baguio's Souvenir Clothes

Don't forget to buy some souvenirs here in Baguio City. There are alot of souvenirs that can be bought and can be a gift to people who you know.

More Foods

Its not just the strawberry, sundot kulangot and peanut brittles that are available but there are more and you name it.

Tam-Awan Village

Tam-awan Village in Pinsao Proper, Baguio City uniquely blends indigenous aesthetics and exquisite Cordilleran craftsmanship with an artist’s concept for a village adapting to Baguio setting. A lot of arts are being showcased here in tam-awan village.

Diplomat Hotel

The Diplomat Hotel is an abandoned property atop Dominican Hill that has gained notoriety as one of the most haunted places in Baguio City and one of the most haunted hotels in the world. But at this time, this place is already been reconstructed inside. People go here to visit this saide haunted place to see if it’s really real.

Burnham Park

Burnham park is the number 1(in our opinion) tourist place here in Baguio City where tourist sit in the grass; they eat and spend time together with there family and relatives. Kids and Kids at heart are also riding bikes there. Rental of bikes there are "want to sawa" every weekdays but in weekends it has time limit because there are alot of people who are there every weekend. People also ride mini boats with different sizes and design. Their rental are also the same as the bikes.

Lourdes Grotto

The Lourdes Grotto is another Catholic shrine and place of meditation in Baguio. It’s a perfect spot to go every holy week.

Ben Cab

Along the asin road, you will see the ben cab. BenCab is short for Benedicto Reyes Cabrera. He is also known as the master of contemporary Philippine art. You will see inside the work of arts that he did. You will see different genre of his arts here.
